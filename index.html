Welcome to J & B Pugs!

We're a family- owned pug breeding business based in Salt lake City, Utah, dedicated to raising healthy, happy pugs. Established in 2020, we have a passion for bringing quality pups to families who are ready to offer them loving, furevever homes. Our focus is on breeding pups that are not only well-socialized but also unique in color and temperament. We take pride in our expertise with "rare" pug colors, ensuring each pup is both distinctive and true to the breed's lovable nature. Whether you'r looking for a new companion or a perfect addidtion to your family, we're here to help you find the perfect pug match!
